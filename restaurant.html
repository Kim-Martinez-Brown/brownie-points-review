Izumi Sushi

All you can eat sushi at a great price. No need to worry though, the quality is not compromised. Fresh fried tempura, good fish thickness, delicious rice, and yummy green matcha ice cream. Great customer service. If you forget to order something, don't worry. You can mark it on the next round.

Mod Pizza

The Mod Pizza location we visited is located on 1604. Customer service was great. We highly recommend the Build Your Own Pizza. No extra cost for the extra amount of toppings on an artisan crust. In this economy, the food to price ratio is so balanced there is no buyer's remorse.
